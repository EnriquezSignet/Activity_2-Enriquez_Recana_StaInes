Welcome to our cat enthusiast's haven!

Cat lovers, unite! The website is dedicated to showing our care for our feline cats. The developers want to inform you about the different breeds of cats and provide you with health tips to help your cat live a long and happy life. The developers also want to help you adopt a beloved cat through The Philippine Animal Welfare Society (PAWS). To show our concern for cats even further, the developers created their own merchandise and will donate a portion and proceeds to PAWS.
So what are you waiting for? Start browsing our website today and show your love for cats!

#FACTS

#FACTS

Cats use their whiskers to “feel” the world around them in an effort to determine which small spaces they can fit into. A cat’s whiskers are generally about the same width as its body.

#FACTS

#FACTS

It’s believed that catnip produces an effect similar to LSD or marijuana in cats. The effects of nepetalactone — the chemical in catnip that can makes cats crazy — wears off within 15 minutes, and won’t surface again for a few hours, even if your cat remains in sniffing distance.

#FACTS

A cat’s purr vibrates at a frequency of 25 to 150 hertz, which is the same frequency at which muscles and bones repair themselves.

#FACTS
