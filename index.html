Who run the world? It's the feline friends!

Cat lovers, unite! The website is dedicated to showing our care for our feline cats. The developers want to inform you about the different breeds of cats and provide you with health tips to help your cat live a long and happy life. The developers also want to help you adopt a beloved cat through The Philippine Animal Welfare Society (PAWS).
To show our concern for cats even further, the developers created their own merchandise and will donate a portion and proceeds to PAWS. So what are you waiting for? Start browsing our website today and show your love for cats!
